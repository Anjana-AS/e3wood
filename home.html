E3 Wood is a Manufacturer of WPC (Wood Polymer composite) products. WPC is an alternative for natural Wood. WPC is composite blend of reclaimed wood (Sawdust), agriculture waste and reclaimed polymer as a binder withfunctional addtives .WPC is an Ecofriendly ,100% recyclable ,termite resistance , water resistance, low maintainence and highly durable.
Wpc will help to control Global warming by avoidcutting trees & control deforesation.
